# Ads & Promotions Intelligence

Advertising campaign management remains out of scope for Version 1.

Ads & Promotions Intelligence is a planned future module. It shall allow users to connect
advertising platforms such as Google Ads and Meta Ads, compare advertising spend with commerce
outcomes, identify promoted products that are underperforming, and recommend where budget may be
increased, reduced, or monitored.

The first implementation shall remain read-only. Salense must not launch campaigns, modify budgets,
change ads, or perform marketplace or advertising write operations.

The current product UI includes an Ads & Promotions placeholder so merchants can understand the
planned direction without being asked for advertising credentials.
