# Salense Product Addendum

## Marketing Intelligence

Status:
Future Module / Coming Soon

Purpose:
Extend Salense from Commerce Intelligence into AI-powered social media and marketing intelligence.

Planned integrations:

- Instagram
- Facebook
- TikTok
- YouTube
- LinkedIn
- X
- Pinterest

Planned capabilities:

- Multi-platform social media analytics
- Audience growth analysis
- Engagement intelligence
- Content performance comparison
- Cross-platform channel comparison
- AI Marketing Briefing
- Risk and opportunity detection
- Action-oriented recommendations
- Future connection between marketing activity and commerce revenue

Product principle:
The module must answer:

1. What happened?
2. Why did it happen?
3. What should I do next?

Architecture principle:
Business reasoning must remain deterministic. The LLM may communicate verified conclusions but must not invent marketing facts or perform unsupported analysis.

Scope:
Read-only intelligence. No publishing, deleting, editing or scheduling social media content in the initial version.
